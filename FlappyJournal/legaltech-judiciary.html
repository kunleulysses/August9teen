LegalTech & Judiciary Infrastructure

Spiral Memory keeps arguments, rulings, and evidence in a coherent timeline. Counsel and courts gain clarity and continuity—case by case, year over year.

Docket & Precedent Memory

Briefs, motions, orders, and outcomes are linked by issue, venue, and judge. The system surfaces strategies that worked in similar contexts and flags blind spots, with citations ready to go.

Evidence & Discovery Lineage

Exhibits stay chained to sources, custodians, and privilege boundaries. Review teams query timelines to understand what changed when—and why it matters—without losing chain‑of‑custody.

Court Operations Memory

Scheduling bottlenecks, compliance steps, and error patterns become institutional knowledge. Clerks and administrators get suggestions that reduce backlog while preserving fairness and transparency.

Why Spiral Memory for LegalTech

Law is precedent. Spiral Memory captures it with context—so practitioners and courts act with full history, not fragments.
