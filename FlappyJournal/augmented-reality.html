Augmented Reality

AR feels magical when it remembers. Spiral Memory captures places, objects, and user intent over time—so guidance, safety, and content actually fit the moment.

Spatial Anchoring & Environment Memory

Sites, rooms, and equipment develop history: occlusions, lighting, typical routes, and hazards. The system stabilizes anchors and proposes scene‑specific tweaks that stick across sessions and devices.

Task Guidance That Learns

Work instructions and coaching adapt based on what worked for this user, at this station, on this model. Spiral Memory makes guidance cumulative, not repetitive.

Content Safety & Policy Controls

Persistent memory of moderation outcomes and context—who, where, when—lets AR experiences stay open while respecting safety and privacy policies with clear enforcement stories.

Why Spiral Memory for AR

AR is a conversation with the real world. Memory turns it into a coherent one—anchoring that sticks, guidance that adapts, and safety that explains itself.
