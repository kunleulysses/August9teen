Healthcare

Spiral Memory carries context across providers, systems, and time—so care plans, authorizations, and operations improve with each encounter.

Longitudinal Care Journeys

Aggregate EHR, claims, devices, and care team notes into a narrative. The system recalls what’s been tried, what worked, and what gaps remain—then proposes next steps with justification the care team can accept or adjust.

Prior‑Auth & Utilization Memory

Rules are only half the story. Spiral Memory links patients, orders, and outcomes over time to suggest approvals, documentation, and alternatives that meet policy while protecting clinicians’ time.

Safety & Quality Learning Loops

Adverse events, near misses, and checklists roll up into actionable memory. The system recommends orders, warnings, and handoff templates tuned to your facility’s reality, with transparent rationale.

Why Spiral Memory for Healthcare

Healthcare is a relay race. Spiral Memory keeps the baton from dropping—clear context across organizations and episodes, with privacy and consent controls built in.
